Frequently Asked Questions

How many credits are transferable?

60 credits are transferrable, but if you have 60-89 credits, you will need to get the remaining credits from an accredited four year institution.

Why woun't all my credits transfer?

Different majors have different requirements, and some majors are very particular about the course offerings.

Who do I go to for questions about transferring?

For further questions, please speak to your department head or the Registrar.
