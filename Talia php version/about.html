Frequently Asked Questions

Disclaimer

Marist College reserves the right to accept or decline a transfer course.
This program should not be taken as completely accurate.
It does not give an official report.
Informational use only.


Do I need to login?

No. Only admins login.


How many credits are transferable?

A maximum of 60 credits, 20 courses, are transferrable at a time.


Why did all of my credits not transfer?

Not all courses transfer.


Why is my course not there?

There is no guarantee that the DCC Courses are up to date.
Additional courses can be sent to Marist Admissions
and they will review and update the listings as appropriate.


Why are some of my courses not visible in the results?

Courses that are not transferable do not show up on the results page.


Who do I contact for questions about transferring?

For further questions, please contact Marist Admissions.


Where do I go to apply to Marist College?

https://www.marist.edu/admission/transfer/
